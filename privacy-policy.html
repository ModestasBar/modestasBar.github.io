Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 24, 2025

MoveMend ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

  • Usage Data: Information about how you use the app, including viewed exercises and feature usage.
  • Device Information: Device type, OS version, and technical identifiers.
  • Cached Data: Exercise videos and content downloaded to your device for offline use.
  • User Preferences: Settings like favorites and video download preferences.

Required Data Collection

To improve app performance and stability, MoveMend collects anonymous usage data and crash reports using Firebase Analytics and Crashlytics. This data helps us understand how the app is used and identify technical issues.

We do not collect personally identifiable information. All exercise data remains stored locally on your device unless deleted by you.

How We Use Your Information

  • To maintain and improve app functionality
  • To analyze app performance and usage trends
  • To identify and fix crashes or errors
  • To provide user support and improve the experience

Data Retention and Deletion

Local data can be deleted from your device using the privacy settings in the app. This includes favorites, downloaded videos, and stored preferences. Anonymous analytics data may remain in aggregate form to improve service quality.

Children’s Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can delete it.

Your Rights

  • Access or delete locally stored app data
  • Withdraw consent for analytics via device settings
